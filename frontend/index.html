FK Flesch-Kincaid Grade
score = 0.39 × (words/sentences) + 11.8 × (syllables/words) - 15.59
WHAT IT MEASURES

Estimates the U.S. school grade level needed to understand the text.

WHEN IT'S MOST ACCURATE

Best for general content — news articles, blog posts, business writing.

SCORE RANGE

0–20+, where lower = easier and higher = more complex.

LIMITATION

Less accurate for highly technical or scientific texts.

SMOG SMOG Index
score = 1.043 × √(complex words × (30 / sentences)) + 3.1291
WHAT IT MEASURES

Estimates years of education needed to understand the text.

WHEN IT'S MOST ACCURATE

Best for health and medical literature.

SCORE RANGE

Typically 6–18, correlating to grade levels.

LIMITATION

Requires at least 30 sentences for accuracy.

FOG Gunning Fog Index
score = 0.4 × ((words/sentences) + 100 × (complex words/words))
WHAT IT MEASURES

Estimates years of formal education needed to read the text on first pass.

WHEN IT'S MOST ACCURATE

Best for business and financial writing.

SCORE RANGE

6–17+, where 12 is ideal for most audiences.

LIMITATION

Overestimates difficulty for texts with many proper nouns.
